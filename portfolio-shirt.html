Company Layout Design

Project Date October - December, 2023
Role Graphic Design, Illustrator
Client ACEM Canada, In Out Concepts UAE and PASE BALLO

The shirt design should be a powerful tool for promoting the company's brand and instilling employee unity or customer enthusiasm. By carefully considering branding elements, design style, and practical aspects, the shirt can effectively convey the company's identity and make a good impression. A well-designed shirt not only increases brand visibility, but also provides comfort and style to those who wear it.

Tailor the design style to the shirt's purpose. For uniforms, choose a clean, professional look with subtle branding. Consider more creative or bold designs that will draw people's attention when designing promotional shirts.
