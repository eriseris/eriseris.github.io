Setting up an e-commerce system for a small business entails creating an online platform that allows the company to sell products or services to customers via the internet. This system must be user-friendly, cost-effective, and scalable to meet the needs of a growing business.

A simple, attractive, and responsive website or app is required. The design should reflect the brand while also providing an intuitive user experience, allowing customers to easily navigate. User accounts, wishlists, and personalized recommendations all contribute to higher levels of customer engagement.
